Code of Conduct

We want Best Practices Summit to be a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive event for everyone. By attending, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

Our Standards

  • Be considerate, respectful, and collaborative.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.
  • Respect privacy and consent for photography and recording.
  • Follow venue rules and staff directions.

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact an organizer immediately at thehackersmeetup@gmail.com or call +91 8980808222. All reports will be handled promptly and confidentially.

Consequences

Participants who violate the Code of Conduct may be warned, asked to leave without a refund, or banned from future events depending on the severity of the incident.
